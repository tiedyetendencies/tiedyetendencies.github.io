A Little About Us

We're glad you're here

Welcome! We're so excited you are here! We want to give you unique, creative, tie dye clothing and accessories at the click of a button! That's why we've built our very own website for you, the user!

Tie Dye Tendencies started as a passion for creative and unique designs. We put that same passion in to each and every one of our products. Whether you're looking for new festival attire, some creative bandanas for a race, or just something plain different, you're in the right place!

We put our very unique dyes and dye patterns on tops, bandanas, beach towels, shoes, backpacks, and so much more! We let you choose the dye patterns and the colors you want on your gear. We give you the option of having your items shipped or picking them up straight from us. We even allow you to bring us items that you want dyed! Keep in mind that cotton items work the best though. We are ready to face any challenge and help bring out your creative side!

Ready to get started? Check out the 'How It Works' section below or just hop right over to our online store to start your order today!

How it Works

You can now even pay for your order online (except for bulk and bring your own orders.) You get to pick whether you want your items shipped or if you want to pick them up!

Whatever your tendency may be, rest assured it's only a click away!
